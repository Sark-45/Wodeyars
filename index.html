Our Story

Welcome to Wodeyars Biriyani, where tradition meets taste. Situated in the vibrant city of Bengaluru, we pride ourselves on serving the most authentic Donne Biryani and traditional Karnataka Nati style dishes.

Our recipes have been passed down through generations, using carefully selected spices roasted to perfection, cooked slowly to bring out the rich, rustic flavors that our patrons have loved for years. Every dish tells a story of Karnataka's rich culinary heritage.

Featured Delicacies

Experience the rich legacy of Nati style cooking.

Mutton Donne Biryani

Tender mutton pieces cooked with seeraga samba rice and our secret blend of ground spices in a traditional style.

Chicken Donne Biryani

Aromatic and flavorful chicken biryani cooked slow and served hot in eco-friendly donne (dried leaves).

Kasturi Chicken

A signature dry starter, marinated in rich green herbs and spices, pan-roasted to absolute perfection.

Parotta & Chilli Chicken

Flaky, multilayered flatbread served rolling hot alongside our spicy and tangy special chilli chicken.
